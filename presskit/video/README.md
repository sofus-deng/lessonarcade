# Demo Video Guide

This guide provides a complete script and shot list for recording a 60-90 second demo video of LessonArcade.

## Video Specifications

- **Duration**: 60-90 seconds
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Audio**: Clear voice narration with background music (optional)

## Recording Setup

### Technical Requirements
- Screen recording software (e.g., OBS, ScreenFlow, Camtasia)
- Microphone for narration
- Stable internet connection
- Browser window sized to 1920x1080
- LessonArcade instance running locally or deployed

### Browser Preparation
1. Clear browser cache and cookies
2. Open developer tools and close them
3. Disable browser extensions
4. Set zoom level to 100%
5. Hide bookmarks and unnecessary UI elements

## Demo Script & Shot List

### Section 1: Introduction (0-15 seconds)

**Shot 1: Title Screen (0-3 seconds)**
- Visual: LessonArcade logo on clean background
- Narration: "Welcome to LessonArcade, where AI-powered voice transforms traditional lessons into interactive experiences."

**Shot 2: Homepage Overview (3-8 seconds)**
- Visual: Scroll through the main demo page
- Action: Show lesson selection interface
- Narration: "Our platform offers a variety of lessons with bilingual support in English and Chinese."

**Shot 3: Language Toggle (8-15 seconds)**
- Visual: Click language toggle to switch between EN/ZH
- Action: Show UI changes for different languages
- Narration: "Seamlessly switch between languages with our intuitive interface."

### Section 2: Voice Lesson Demo (15-45 seconds)

**Shot 4: Selecting a Lesson (15-20 seconds)**
- Visual: Click on "Effective Meetings" lesson
- Action: Navigate to lesson detail page
- Narration: "Let's explore our voice-enabled lesson on effective meetings."

**Shot 5: Voice Player Interface (20-30 seconds)**
- Visual: Show the voice player controls
- Action: Highlight play, pause, and stop buttons
- Narration: "Each lesson features AI-powered narration with full playback controls."

**Shot 6: Voice Playback (30-38 seconds)**
- Visual: Click play button and show audio playing
- Action: Show visual indicator of audio playback
- Narration: "Experience natural-sounding AI voices that bring lessons to life."

**Shot 7: Voice Controls Demo (38-45 seconds)**
- Visual: Demonstrate pause and resume functionality
- Action: Show how users can control playback
- Narration: "Users have complete control over their learning experience with pause, resume, and replay options."

### Section 3: Analytics & Studio (45-75 seconds)

**Shot 8: Studio Access (45-50 seconds)**
- Visual: Navigate to /studio route
- Action: Show Basic Auth login screen
- Narration: "For administrators, our studio provides powerful analytics and insights."

**Shot 9: Analytics Dashboard (50-60 seconds)**
- Visual: Show voice analytics dashboard
- Action: Scroll through completion rates and metrics
- Narration: "Track engagement with comprehensive analytics including completion rates and user interaction patterns."

**Shot 10: Advanced Features (60-70 seconds)**
- Visual: Show top interruption points and language breakdown
- Action: Highlight key metrics
- Narration: "Identify popular content and optimize lessons with detailed usage analytics."

**Shot 11: Technical Highlights (70-75 seconds)**
- Visual: Brief glimpse of rate limiting in action
- Action: Show error handling or rate limit message
- Narration: "Built with robust guardrails including rate limiting and intelligent caching."

### Section 4: Conclusion (75-90 seconds)

**Shot 12: Benefits Summary (75-82 seconds)**
- Visual: Return to homepage
- Action: Show key features overview
- Narration: "LessonArcade combines cutting-edge AI voice technology with powerful analytics to create engaging educational experiences."

**Shot 13: Call to Action (82-90 seconds)**
- Visual: LessonArcade logo with contact information
- Action: Fade to black with website URL
- Narration: "Transform your lessons today with LessonArcade. Visit our website to learn more."

## Production Notes

### Visual Guidelines
- **Smooth Transitions**: Use gentle fades between shots
- **Cursor Visibility**: Keep cursor visible but not distracting
- **Highlight Actions**: Use subtle zoom or emphasis for key interactions
- **Consistent Speed**: Maintain steady pace throughout

### Audio Guidelines
- **Clear Narration**: Speak clearly and at moderate pace
- **Background Music**: Optional subtle music during transitions
- **Sound Effects**: Minimal sound effects for UI interactions
- **Volume Balance**: Ensure narration is clearly audible

### Post-Production
- **Color Correction**: Ensure consistent colors throughout
- **Text Overlays**: Add text for key points if needed
- **Progress Bar**: Include progress indicator at bottom
- **Branding**: Add logo watermark in corner (optional)
